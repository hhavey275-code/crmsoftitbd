

## Favicon Update Plan

The uploaded image (a blue/black logo) will be set as the favicon for the site.

### Steps

1. **Copy the uploaded image** to `public/favicon.png`
2. **Update `index.html`** to reference the new favicon with a `<link rel="icon">` tag pointing to `/favicon.png`

This will apply to both the Lovable preview and the Vercel deployment after the next push.
